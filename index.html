Pioneering Physicist & Chemist

Hello, my name is Marie Curie

I am a physicist and chemist known for my groundbreaking research on radioactivity. My discoveries led to the development of X-ray technology and advancements in cancer treatment. As the first woman to win a Nobel Prize—and the only person to win in two different scientific fields—I dedicated my life to science and humanity.
